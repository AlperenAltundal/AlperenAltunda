About

Hello, I'm Ahmet Alperen ALTUNDAL. I am a 4th year student at Sakarya University of Applied Sciences. I was born in Kocaeli and lived in this city all my life. I'm studying in the department of electrical and electronics engineering at my university and I hope to graduate this year. At first, I chose the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering with great enthusiasm. But as time passed, I aimed to improve myself in the field of software. When I was 18, I was working on writing code as an amateur, but in my second year of university, I started to develop my first steps in image processing with python. Of course, even though I can't use all my free time, I thought of giving importance to software before I started my 3rd year at university. And my priority was to learn a software language and develop my algorithm logic, to develop and implement that language. I also started learning the software language called C#. As much as I wanted to learn, I had other responsibilities when I did these, so I had other responsibilities for about 5 months, albeit intermittently on C#. I worked on the parts I needed. While I was thinking about what I wanted to do afterwards, the idea of making a game with the unity game engine came to my mind, and I started learning unity. After learning unity, I made a 2D Platform Game for myself.



When the university started the 4th year, we had to do a graduation project and we chose a Deep Learning-based project with our friends, of course, we chose this project at the end of the 3rd year of the university and I started my studies on these subjects at my internship place in the summer. After receiving training in many subjects such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning, we presented a concrete example where I made almost the entire design project myself. Now, during the break, we had a thought to control this project from the interface environment and I did it using the tkinter library in python. Then I learned the HTML CSS Javascript concepts that I always wanted to learn before, and the HTML CSS Javascript concepts that I always wanted to learn. and meanwhile, there was a very sad and deeply affecting earthquake in our country. I wish everyone a good, healthy, happy and peaceful life.
